What We Have Achieved

Our achievements reflect strong partnerships, sustainable practices, and large-scale execution of green and renewable energy projects focused on reducing carbon footprints.

Strategic Green Partnerships

Tied up with multiple entities involved in green and renewable energy projects, actively working towards reducing carbon footprints through the use of sustainable and eco-friendly materials.

EPC Turnkey Contracts

Executing EPC contracts on a turnkey basis with 09 companies to develop 10 TPD CBG manufacturing units, utilizing 80% press mud and 20% Napier grass as base raw materials.

Raw Material Supply Network

Established long-term raw material supply contracts with farmers for Napier grass and with sugar industries for press mud, ensuring consistent and sustainable input supply for every project.

IOCL Letter of Intent (LOI)

Received Letter of Intent (LOI) from Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) for each project for the selling of Compressed Bio-Gas (CBG), strengthening commercial viability and long-term offtake assurance.
