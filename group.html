Venturiq Group

Overview

Venturiq Group is committed to developing sustainable energy solutions with a focus on Bio-CNG and renewable fuels. With India rapidly urbanizing, the need for cleaner air and sustainable energy has never been greater. Natural Gas and Renewable Energy offer an eco-friendly alternative, reducing harmful emissions and supporting a healthier future for both people and the environment.

Our Project

Venturiq Group specializes in setting up Bio-CNG plants on a turnkey basis. Our cluster-based model ensures self-sufficient production and distribution of compressed bio-gas, covering every stage from feedstock management to dispensing stations. By leveraging advanced technology, we maximize gas yield from agricultural residues and biomass, contributing to a cleaner and greener energy ecosystem.
