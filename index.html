About Us

Who We Are

We are a forward-thinking clean energy company dedicated to driving India's transition towards a sustainable future through the development and implementation of Bio-CNG (Compressed Natural Gas from biomass) projects. With a sharp focus on innovation, reliability, and environmental stewardship, we specialize in delivering end-to-end turnkey solutions for Bio-CNG plants nationwide. Our expertise spans the entire project lifecycle—from conceptualization, design, engineering, procurement, and construction, to commissioning and operations support. Leveraging cutting-edge technologies and a skilled multidisciplinary team, we ensure our clients receive efficient, cost-effective, and future-ready Bio-CNG infrastructure tailored to their specific needs.
We work closely with stakeholders across the public and private sectors, aligning with national sustainability goals and contributing to a cleaner, greener, and energy-secure India.

Vision

To be the leading force in delivering sustainable energy infrastructure by providing world class project management solutions for renewable energy projects. We envision the future when clean energy is accessible, efficient and scalable driven by our commitment to innovation, environment responsibility and operational excellence.

Mission

To develop and deliver cost effective, safe and clean solutions that will maximize prosperity of our customers to be a responsible corporate citizen.
