Health at Venturiq Group

This policy aims to safeguard and promote employee health, recognizing it as essential to productivity and organizational effectiveness. It focuses on identifying and controlling health risks, providing timely medical care, and ensuring both physical and mental well-being. Compliance with legal standards and regular health check-ups are key components, along with awareness programmes on substance abuse. The policy also emphasizes protective measures in hazardous environments and regular reviews to track progress.

Safety at Venturiq Group

At venturiq group, we believe that it is possible to fuel the energy needs of the Nation while also protecting people and the environment. We are committed to conducting business in a manner that is compatible with the environmental and economic needs of the communities in which we operate, and that protects the safety and health of our employees, those involved with our operations, our customers and the public.
